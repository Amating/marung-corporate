The Story

One moment. Two years of work.

In April 2025, our founder received money via FNB eWallet. It arrived digitally — on her phone, in seconds. And then the system did what it always does to millions of South Africans: it forced her back to cash.

"The moment money arrived on my phone and I was told to go to an ATM — that was the moment Marung was built. Two years later, it is a registered company, a working prototype, and a movement."

— Madichaba Milosevic · Founder & Director · Marung Sebaka Technologies (Pty) Ltd

Marung Sebaka Technologies was incorporated in March 2026. Marung is a Sesotho word meaning clouds. Sebaka means place. A place in the clouds — accessible to everyone, from anywhere, on any phone.

The FNB Research

R43 billion. 69 million transactions.All forced back to cash.

South African banks collectively process over R43 billion in cash-send-to-mobile-number transactions annually across all major banks. FNB alone reported 69 million eWallet transactions worth R43 billion in one year. Every single sender is a South African bank account holder. Every single recipient is forced to exit the banking ecosystem by withdrawing cash. Marung changes one thing: the money stays digital.

This is not an FNB problem. It is the architecture of the entire SA informal payment system. Every bank operates in its own silo. Millions of people receive money digitally and are immediately pushed back to cash. Marung is the layer that ends this.
The Product

One number. Any bank.Money stays digital.

Marung is not a bank. Marung is not an app. Marung is a regulated orchestration and distribution layer — connecting South Africa's fragmented bank silos to one mobile number.

How It Works

Four steps. That is the whole journey.

📱
Dial a code — any phone

*120*MARUNG# from any basic feature phone on any SA network. No app. No data. No smartphone. Wallet created in under a minute.

💸
Receive from any SA bank

Anyone with a bank account sends to your Marung number. FNB, Standard Bank, Capitec, Absa, Nedbank — all accepted. Money arrives and stays digital.

💳
Spend anywhere

Virtual Mastercard included. Pay at any shop, online or in-store, informal traders and spaza shops via QR code.

🌍
Send home — R0 fee

Lesotho, Mozambique, Zimbabwe and more — included in R10/month. No percentage. No hidden charges. Ever.
